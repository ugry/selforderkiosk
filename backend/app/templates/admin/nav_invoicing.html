NAV Online Számla (Online Invoice System) is the Hungarian tax authority's mandatory e-invoicing reporting system. Every VAT invoice issued in Hungary must be reported in real time (or within 4 days for paper invoices). This integration reports completed orders as B2C paper invoices.
